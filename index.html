Billionaire Pity Party is a sketch comedy team based out of Los Angeles, CA. The byproduct of USC, UCLA, and inattentive parents, you can catch us every fifth Thursday at the Pack Theater, or at Whole Foods failing to cope with adulthood.

BPP has been featured in midwestern journalism heavyweights such as the Chicago Tribune and Time Out Chicago
